Attendance

Attendance data of all students is maintained online.By using our platform not only maintaining data but accessing data is made easy.

Tracking Of Work

A diary is maintained for each student making a record of all the activities done in classwork and homework and input for both the work sections is taken from respected teachers.

Assignment management

Assignments, projects, etc can be conducted and can be auto-approved or manually approved and can also leave private feedback to each student.

Grades

Report card for students is maintained virtually. A virtual signature, OTP, fingerprint or face recognition can confirm that the parent has seen and has knowledge of the grades.

Advanced Quizzing

The results of quizzes and tests can be made online. Preparation, evaluation, grade/rank assessment is made easy for teachers by maintaining it online.

Fees

Fee can be paid by the parent/guardian through online. Receipts and due statements can be maintained online and can be generated instantly.

Feedback

A feedback section will be made available which enables the chat options to review the students performance by teachers. Feedback on teachers is taken care of by officials through online forms.

Tracking of buses

The live location of the bus is shared by using the GPS location of Bus driver, which helps parents prepare students for school and ensures their security during the journey.

Extracurricular activities

Videos of the recorded sessions, seminars, live sessions will be shared through the platform to enhance the interest of students in different fields. These sessions can be revisited whenever required.

Push Notifications

Notifications regarding homework, assignments, and other deadlines are sent to corresponding students.
